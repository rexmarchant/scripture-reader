Audio Source — Archive.org
Archive.org provides free, permanent, streamable MP3 hosting. To get your direct URL:
1. Go to archive.org/upload and upload your MP3.
2. After upload, open the item page and find your file under the download section.
3. Right-click the filename → Copy link address.
✗ Wrong: archive.org/details/your-item
✓ Correct: archive.org/download/your-item/your-file.mp3
Or use a relative path (e.g. audio/file.mp3) if hosting audio on Netlify.
